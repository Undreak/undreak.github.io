About Me

Experimental physicist with an international dual Master's degree in Physics of Soft Matter from Université Paris Cité and Complex Materials from Universidad de La Coruña, obtained through a collaborative Erasmus+ program.

My research focuses on soft matter physics with a specialization in polymer dynamics and scattering techniques (SAXS/SANS, DLS). I combine rigorous experimental methods with computational modeling using Python and C/C++ to investigate complex phenomena at the nanoscale.

Currently preparing for doctoral school entrance exams, I'm passionate about understanding the fundamental behavior of soft materials through both experiment and theory. Beyond physics, I maintain a broad intellectual curiosity spanning philosophy, history, and analytical thinking across disciplines.
